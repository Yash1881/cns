About Us

Our mission is to empower and support our community by connecting people to the resources they need.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, the Community Support Network began as a small group of volunteers dedicated to helping those in need. Over the years, we have grown into a thriving organization with numerous programs that provide essential support services to individuals and families in our community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a supportive network that empowers people to overcome challenges and thrive. By offering resources, counseling, and volunteer opportunities, we strive to create a community where everyone has access to the help they need.
